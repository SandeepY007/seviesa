MODULAR KITCHEN, MODULAR WARDROBE & MODULAR BEDS FOR NEW AGE HOMES

Saviesa is founded and run hands-on by pioneers in the field of modular kitchens and modular wardrobes in India. As one of India's leading modular kitchen manufacturers, Saviesa has mastered its expertise and wishes to pass this knowledge on to anyone who is keen to learn. Hence, our tagline is 'Guidance through Mastery'.
We, the Saviesa, are focused on your needs and aspirations for your dream kitchens and wardrobes. We understand your functional and emotional needs and record them in our specially developed digital apps… then, using modern design, we tailor-make a fusion of beautiful form and brilliant function at a cost that suits your needs.

With several youngsters making up the Indian population, there has been a great demand for housing in metro cities. And since the well-paid millennials and Generation Z want to stay closer to their workplace at the city's heart, it has led to the trend of compact housing. The limited space and the exorbitant prices of living space in metro cities introduced developers to the challenge of providing intelligently designed close housing. Amazingly, in only the past ten years, projects with 1RK, 1.5RK, and 1BHK units have gained immense traction. These concepts have shown us home design creativity, which remains high on utility and low on compromise. It further boosted the idea of intelligent interior design and space-saving furniture ideas.

There are a lot of great interior design companies in Mumbai which have witnessed this trend of compact housing from its very beginning. It has given them the expertise to deal with any interior design and modular furniture requirements efficiently. Regarding your home, you'd want a team of design experts who will understand your needs. They must be able to visualize what you want and, most importantly, fuse your opinions into their design ideas. Our design experts at Saviesa do just that for you. When you call us to visit your home, our experts will measure each dimension of a room. They will discuss the pain points to be solved with the new design. You can virtually create the design and review all the fits and finishes on our proprietary design application.

As one of the leading interior design companies and modular kitchen manufacturers in India, Saviesa offers a wide range of materials, finishes, appliances, hardware, lights, and shutters for your furniture. You will not have to worry about dirt, dust, and noise in your home as your modular furniture will be produced in a factory. The result is a stylish, comfortable, efficient and functional home.

At Saviesa, we offer a range of modular options from your kitchen to the bedroom and even turnkey interior design solutions for your complete home. Here are some more details of what you can achieve with modular designs.

• Modular Kitchen:

For new-age compact homes, modular kitchen manufacturers in India are a blessing. These spaces can be tailor-made according to the dimensions of the kitchen area and to suit the home's overall aesthetic. A modular kitchen will allow ample storage units and sufficient counter space and refresh your kitchen rather than stuffy.

• Modular Wardrobe:

A modular wardrobe for your bedroom offers you the ability to customize the space inside the frame. At Saviesa, you can choose from storage trays, drawers, rails, hangers and show racks for your wardrobe. Wardrobes also offer mobility, which means they can be moved from one room to another. How you design your modular wardrobe can change the complete look and feel of the bedroom.

• Intelligent Modular Beds:

With the virtue of having small bedrooms, intelligent modular beds are a necessity. You can choose to have dual-purpose modular beds which can fold away to turn into a table or a work desk. There are also wall unit designs which offer storage along with fold-away beds. The more straightforward option is beds with storage beneath them.

• Modular Kitchen Designs:

As one of the best modular kitchen manufacturers in Mumbai, we offer four modular kitchen designs to our customers. If you have a narrow kitchen room, our design experts suggest a parallel modular kitchen design that provides the benefit of two kitchen counters. A well-designed parallel modular kitchen can look very contemporary and give the narrow room an airy feel. One can opt for an L-shaped modular kitchen design in an open-layout compact home. A modular kitchen island is versatile because it can be used in a big kitchen room and even an open-layout home. The kitchen island can be customized to fit in any space. There is also a U-shaped modular kitchen design, which is ideal for larger kitchen rooms.

• Modular Bedroom Design -

In new-age compact bedrooms, it is essential to achieve a comfortable feeling. Modular bedroom design is where intelligent use of shelves, storage units, lighting, and dual-purpose furniture is made. Modular bedroom designs allow space saving for enough leg space to move around quickly.

• Modular Wardrobe Design -

Whether a small bedroom or a big bedroom with extra space inside it, modular wardrobe designs are available for all. A regular wardrobe is as efficient, functional and stylish as a fancy walk-in closet. These options can be designed with the end user's style and usage. Modular wardrobe designs offer the opportunity to choose the materials and finishes and the storage units, shelves, hangers, rails and shoe racks. Saviesa is an expert in creating bespoke interiors. Your custom-made modular furniture is an extension of your style. Just like the paint and tiles of your home match your taste and style, your modular furniture is a great way to add to it.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • What is a modular kitchen?

    The term 'Modular Kitchen', as the name suggests, is derived from the word module which means individual unit. Modular kitchen furniture layouts consist of modules (units) of cabinets which can be arranged according to one's preference and hence the term 'modular kitchen'.

  • What are the advantages of a modular kitchen?

    1. Clean, modern, and trendy look

    2. Different modules of different sizes can be achieved

    3. Provides proper space and storage management

    4. Designed as per the dynamics of the room

    5. Easy to Install, maintain & repair

    6. Fewer efforts in cleaning

    7. Easily moved to your new home

    8. Modular kitchens are a style statement

    9. Wide range of finishes

  • Can you customise a design to any kitchen shape?

    Yes, you can select any design based on your kitchen shape. Thanks to our app 'Saviesa Dreamviewer' you can customize your kitchen in the virtual world by selecting from various kitchen layouts, cabinets, floors, countertops, and appliances.

  • Are modular kitchens durable?

    Most conventional kitchen cabinets are wooden, which makes them prone to attack from termites and other borers. Most of them use high-quality plywood that is resistant to boiling water and undergo treatment to allow them to handle moisture better. While some cabinets use cheap MDF and particle board instead of plywood, reputed modular kitchen manufacturers take additional care to ensure that the kitchen is easy to maintain and durable. Some of the additional measures they take to make the kitchen last longer include lining the cabinet doors with aluminium, especially in water-prone areas such as sink cabinets. While the cabinet will match the rest of the units, it will have added protection to make it more durable.

  • Is there any warranty on Saviesa modular kitchens?

    Yes, there is a warranty of 7 years on the kitchen and a life-time warranty on accessories.

  • What are the 6 types of kitchen layouts?

    1. Straight shaped

    2. L-shaped

    3. Parallel shaped/ Galley shaped

    4. C/ U-shaped

    5. G-shaped/ Peninsula shaped

    6. Island shaped

  • What is the difference between a modular kitchen and a regular kitchen?

    1. Modular kitchens are made up of separate modules. If necessary, a part of the kitchen can also be replaced. In normal kitchens, however, such replacement is not possible.

    2. Installation of a modular kitchen does not take much time or effort. It comes flat-packed in boxes and is installed at the site with minimal noise, dust or mess. Regular kitchens, on the other hand, take a lot of time and have to be installed at the site from scratch.

    3. The hardware runs smoothly on the modular carcass as compared to stone verticals in normal kitchens.

    4. Modular kitchens are portable. You can easily carry the cabinet in a box and fit the same in your new kitchen. Normal kitchens cannot be dismantled and moved to your new home.

    5. In modular kitchens you can create 25% more space as compared to a regular kitchen, as there will be better utilization of your corner spaces.

  • What is an L-shaped kitchen?

    The L- shaped kitchen is the most popular design and is appropriate for any size kitchen. It includes workspace on two adjoining walls running perpendicular to each other.

  • Advantages of an L-shaped modular kitchen?

    1. When it comes to a good balance between storage and functionality, there's nothing like an L-shaped kitchen.

    2. This layout works well for two people working at the same time since no traffic lanes flow through the work area.

    3. This layout provides a more efficient area to the user.

  • What are the different variations in L-shaped kitchens?

    1. L-shaped kitchens can have one wall made of a succession of tall cabinets and the other containing a battery of appliances, including the cooktop and sinks.

    2. L-shaped kitchens can have a continuous work surface at the corner between the two walls and corner cabinetry.

    3. L-shaped kitchens can have a panoramic view when the lower cabinets of the L create a work surface running below a window, offering a view while preparing food.

    4. L-shaped kitchens are characterised by an aesthetic solution that adopts panels and shelves to link the elements present on the two adjoining walls.

  • What is a U-shaped modular kitchen?

    In a U-shaped kitchen, the countertop, overhead cabinets, and base cabinets run along three adjacent walls. The kitchen gives the most efficient work triangle equation.

  • What are the pros of the U-shaped modular kitchen?

    1. The U-shaped kitchen gives some extra square footage.

    2. If planned well, this layout will ensure that users do not have to walk around a lot between cabinets and appliances.

  • What are the accessories you can use in a modular kitchen?

    1. Drawer organizers

    2. Pull out accessories

    3. Corner accessories

    4. Midway accessories

    5. Wall accessories

    6. Tall accessories

  • Can a modular kitchen be shifted?

    Yes. The main advantage of a modular kitchen is that you can easily carry your cabinets (modules )in a box and fit the same in your new kitchen.

  • Can modular kitchens be attached to fixed/existing platforms?

    If you plan to retain your existing platform, the biggest advantage of a modular kitchen is that the modules can be customised to fit into your existing kitchen. Accessories in fixed sizes can be fitted inside the kitchen unit. If your kitchen already comes with supports, sinks, and cabinetry, it will be difficult to fit modular kitchen units into it. By providing proper measurements of the kitchen, one can deliver a standardised kitchen for you.

  • How much time does it take to setup a modular kitchen?

    Usually, 5 days are required for modular kitchen setup. Modular kitchen assembly takes 2 days but 3 days are kept as spare for any unexpected changes to be done due to the setup. Whereas, to renovate a basic kitchen to a semi-modular kitchen, the job can be completed in about 12-15 days.
